Proposal: Give Us 2×–3× More Dailies and Weeklies from Level 1

Pitch:
With no questlines at wipe start and minimal structure, players are feeling directionless. Instead of quest chains, BSG could introduce double or triple the number of daily and weekly tasks, available from level 1.

Why It Makes Sense

Immediate goals without bringing quests back (yet)

Dailies tasks instantly create short‑term objectives, making early wipe gameplay feel purposeful rather than aimless.

Accessible to all playstyles

Gives new, casual and hardcore players simple/achievable objectives that offer mid-tier rewards.

Suggested Rewards: Let’s Have Some Fun

Rewards could include:

  • Roubles — 100‑300k
  • Pre‑built mid‑tier weapons (e.g., AKs, MP5s, M4A1s), no 50 rounders!
  • Class 3/4 armour pieces for a satisfying power spike
  • Hideout Items another way to earn items required to upgrade hideout stations
Even if we’re “gonna lose it anyway,” getting to toy with some halfway decent gear gives the wipe flavour and memorability.
